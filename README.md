# Baskin-Robbins-Sales
##   project Overview
This project presents a Sales Performance Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze and visualize the business performance of a Baskin Robbins outlet network. The dashboard provides insights into sales trends, order patterns, payment methods, category performance, and seasonal analysis, helping stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

## Business Objective
To analyze:

- Total Sales Performance 
- Average Grand Total
- Sales Percent by Category
- Payment Mode Distribution
- Yearly & Quarterly Sales Trends
- Total sales by year
- Count on Payment mode by Quarter 
  
## Key KPIs
- Total Stores
- Total Sales
- Total Orders
- Average Grand Total

## Analytical Highlights
🔹 Revenue Distribution by Payment Mode

- The dashboard analyzes UPI, Digital Wallet, Cash, Card, and Gift Coupons contributions, enabling insights into evolving customer payment preferences.
  
🔹 Total sales by year
 - Identify high-performing years,Understand sales fluctuations,Make data-driven decisions for future sales strategies

🔹 Category-Level Performance

- Ice Cream Cakes and Family Packs emerge as major revenue contributors, offering insights into high-margin product segments.
  
🔹 Yearly & Quarterly Trend Analysis

- A time-series view of revenue helps identify seasonal fluctuations and growth consistency across financial periods.
